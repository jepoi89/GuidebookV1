Settings

Guidebook Studio runs entirely offline. Projects, images, and files are stored in this browser's local database (IndexedDB) and never leave your device.

Use Download My Progress regularly to back up all of your projects to a single file, and Import My Progress to restore or move them to another computer.

Clearing your browser's site data for this app will permanently delete saved projects, so keep a backup if that matters to you.
